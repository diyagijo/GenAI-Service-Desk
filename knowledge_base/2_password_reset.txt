KB-Article: PWD-004
Title: How to Reset Your Corporate Password

1.  **Self-Service Portal:** The fastest way to reset your password is via the Self-Service Portal at: https://reset.my-company-portal.com
2.  **Requirements:** Your new password MUST be at least 14 characters long, include one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character (e.g., !, @, #).
3.  **Restrictions:** You cannot reuse any of your previous 5 passwords.
4.  **Lockout Policy:** If you enter your password incorrectly 5 times, your account will be locked for 30 minutes.
5.  **Support:** If you are locked out or cannot answer your security questions, you must call the IT Help Desk for a manual reset. Do not email for a password reset.
